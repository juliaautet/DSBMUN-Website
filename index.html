Dear MUN directors and faculty advisors,

The MUN team of the German School of Barcelona proudly announces its first Model United Nations Conference, DSBMUN, from January 19th – 21st 2023, with hopes of many more to come. The conference is open to delegates between the ages of 14 and 19 and aimed at experienced delegates as well as complete newcomers. It will be held on our school premises in Esplugues de Llobregat, near Barcelona.

We hope DSBMUN will encourage all attending delegates to discuss different topics politcal stabilization. There will be a fee of 50.- Euros per delegate and MUN director, covering all materials as well as food and drinks during the conference. If your school wishes to participate, please fill out this form. Please note that the deadline for signing up is Sunday 4th December. We're looking forward to seeing you in January!

Best regards,

The DSBMUN team
