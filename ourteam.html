Our Team

Stella Tarradelles

Secretary General

Stella Tarradellas is a Secretary General and chair of UNODC for this year’s DSBMUN conference. She is currently 16 in her junior year of high school. Stella started participating in MUN in 8th grade, attending various in-person conferences such as BFMUN, BCNMUN, HAGAMUN, and WAWMUN. Growing up in a Greek-Spanish household, she grew interested in the way multiple cultures can grow together. That’s why she joined MUN: Through this club, she has developed her public speaking skills and has been given the opportunity to meet amazing people. Stella is looking forward to chair this April and is hoping for a fun, yet productive debate in UNODC.

Anastasia Salvat

Secretary General

Anastasia Salvat is the Secretary General of DSBMUN 2024 and a chair of the Security Council. Half Spanish and half Russian-Lithuanian, she was born in Barcelona and has lived all of her life here. She is attending her junior year of high school and has been in MUN since 8th grade. After participating in conferences such as HAGAMUN, WAWMUN, BCNMUN, and BFMUN, Anastasia has developed an inmense passion for debating, solving worldwide issues, and public speaking. Being in the executive team has also helped her to enhance leadership skills and entail her responsibilities. MUN has given her life-lasting friendships, unforgettable memories, and extraordinary opportunities. She is hoping for a fun and fruitful conference.

Alina González de Mesa

USG of Finance

Alina González is this year's Under Secretary General of Finance and chair in UNODC. She has lived in Barcelona for her entire life and is currently in her junior year at DSB. Attending various conferences, such as BCNMUN, WAWMUN, and HAGAMUN, and being a member of her school's MUN for over three years has helped her develop her public speaking skills and has given her a high passion for debating. Through MUN, she has gotten the chance to meet people from all over the world and make new friends. She looks forward to seeing fun and fruitful debates and wishes all delegates the best of luck!

Julia Martin

They have a passion for public speaking, debating, and socializing, which they further developed by attending conferences such as BCNMUN, HAGAMUN, BFMUN, and WAWMUN. MUN has helped them grow confident in themselves as well as meet interesting people from all around the world. Julia is looking forward to the conference and wishes everyone the best of luck!

Alejandro Novellón

USG of Media

Alejandro Novellón is the Under Secretary General of Media of DSBMUN 2024. Half Spanish and half German, he was born in Barcelona and has lived his whole life there. He is 17 years old and in the 11th Grade. He has been part of the executive team since last year. He was the photographer of last year's conference and the designer of the old webpage. Since he was little, he loves to be surrounded by people and combining it with his passion: Technology. He is so excited to bring his passion for Model UN and her love of photography together to make DSBMUN an amazing event for all delegates!

Júlia Autet García

USG of Technology

Júlia Autet García is this year's Under Secretary General of Technology and a chair in ECOSOC. She has spent her entire life in Barcelona, where she was born. Currently in her seventh year at the German School of Barcelona, she is in tenth grade. In the eighth grade, Júlia became involved in MUN due to her passion for global issues, debating, public speaking, and geopolitics. Júlia has participated in various MUN conferences, including BCNMUN, DSBMUN, HAGAMUN, BFMUN, and WAWMUN, both virtually and in person, throughout the world. She hopes that ECOSOC will provide an opportunity for both new and seasoned delegates to participate in a heated but exciting debate during this year's DSBMUN. Júlia hopes that this conference will be an unforgettable event.

Leo Martin

Debate Coordinator

Magdalena Burghart

USG of Public Relations
